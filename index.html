About

I am a graduate student at the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA), majoring in Control Theory and Control Engineering. My research interests lie in intelligent assessment and rehabilitation for motor–cognitive functions in older adults, as well as AI and neural network modeling for health informatics. I am currently under the supervision of Hongjun Yang . I'm always looking for related collaboration. If you are interested to chat with me, feel free to drop me an email.
